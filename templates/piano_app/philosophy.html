Philosophy

  • Piano education should provide an innovative, efficient, and enjoyable way for young children to learn to read music notation and to play competently with both hands.
  • An emphasis on focusing on the notation, following the instructor, and playing with flow empowers young pianists to make faster progress in their development.
  • We should contribute to the musical education of others and serve in our communities as volunteers and leaders.
  • We should employ our talents to reach out to underprivileged, vulnerable, or marginalized groups whenever possible.

What is Felicia3EFM?

3: Parents, Student, and Teacher form a triangular team. Across different learning stages, the three key terms represent the principle approach on that stage. For example:
E: Energy, Engage, Efficiency
F: Focus, Follow, Flow
M: Mindset, Manner, Motivation
